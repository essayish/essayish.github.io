Fall 2020

Index

  1. Being
    1. The Law
      1. Strength: the greater overcomes the weaker
      2. Beauty: the balanced lasts while the imbalanced fades away
      3. Goodness: appropriate or compatible, such that things work
    2. Chaos
      1. Frailty: the lesser overcomes what's bigger than it
      2. Horror: disproportionate or patternless stands up while the even thing falls down
      3. Evil: the discordent fits in the working order while the harmonious stops
  2. Having
    1. Power
      1. Wealth: something stored away
      2. Joy: something at hand where prior there was nothing
      3. Honor: the promise of something that does not belong, is neither stored away or at hand
    2. Void
      1. Poverty: lacking something and never having it
      2. Pain: the loss of something, leaving an empty space
      3. Shame: debt, or the promise of loss, then lack
  3. Doing
    1. Love
      1. Friendship: things for each other, as though moving in the same direction
      2. Romance: when things come together as one
      3. Parenthood: giving, like the passing of something on to another
    2. Death
      1. Enmity: things against one another, as though moving in opposite directions
      2. Violence: things falling apart
      3. Enslavement: taking something for use, like a tool
